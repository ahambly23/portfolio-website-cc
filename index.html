About Me

Hey! I’m Ashley, a 26-year-old coder from Birmingham with a passion for crafting engaging digital experiences. When I’m not buried in code, you’ll find me soaking up the thrill of the sports world—whether that’s cheering on my favorite football team or tuning in for every high-speed twist and turn of Formula 1. Gaming is another big part of my life, fueling both my competitive edge and my love for all things tech. From tackling complex coding challenges to immersing myself in new gaming worlds, I thrive on a good challenge and the satisfaction of a project well done.

I started my coding journey as a curious hobby, and it quickly evolved into an all-out passion. Now, I’m all about pushing the limits, experimenting with new ideas, and bringing creativity into my work every chance I get. My approach to projects is part tech, part art—finding solutions that don’t just work well but look and feel great. I’m excited about where the web can take us and love being part of an industry that’s always changing. If you’re looking for someone with a bit of grit, a lot of enthusiasm, and an eye for detail, let’s connect and create something amazing!
